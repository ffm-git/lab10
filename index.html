Train

You can find a small train track running through the park which is there for children to ride. The train can be seen in the picture on the right. I remember riding this train when I was younger and even though the track is only 0.5 miles long, it felt like a long journey as a child.

Birthday BBQ

As the park is open to the public and my birthday is in July, I used to celebrate my birthday in the Volkspark Mainz where you can play soccer or have a barbeque. It was a lot of fun for me to invite all my friends to come to the park.

Family wedding

The park has to offer a restaurant and a minigolf course as well so my family rented the place for a huge family wedding where I had fun with all my cousins.

About the Volkspark

The Volkspark Mainz is a park in the city of Mainz in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. It is open to the public all year and was created in 1935 on the historic area of Fort Weisenau. There is a small train track running through the park, that was remade in 1962 when the city of Mainz celebrated its 2000-year anniversary.
